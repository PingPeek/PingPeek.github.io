Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Core commitment: All network traffic data processed by NetTweak is handled locally on your device and is never uploaded to any external server.

1. Information We Collect

NetTweak does not collect any personally identifiable information. Data stored locally on your device includes:

  • Network traffic statistics (upload/download bytes, real-time speed)
  • Network simulation parameters configured by the user (bandwidth limit, latency, packet loss rate, etc.)
  • Traffic session records (stored in a local SQLite database on your device)

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • Contents or payloads of your network requests
  • URLs or domain names you visit
  • Account information, names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers
  • Device location information

3. VPN Permission

NetTweak uses the Android VPN Service API to create a local virtual network interface (tun interface) for intercepting, analyzing, and simulating network conditions entirely within your device.

This mechanism runs entirely locally and does not forward any traffic to external proxies or servers. The VPN permission is used solely to enable network simulation and traffic statistics features, and does not require root access.

4. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored in your device's private app directory (protected by the Android sandbox) and is inaccessible to other apps. Data never leaves your device through any channel.

5. Third-Party Services

NetTweak integrates Firebase, which is used solely to collect app crash reports and performance data. This integration does not collect any user data or personally identifiable information.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for developers and technical users. It is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Policy Changes

If this policy undergoes significant changes, we will notify users through app update release notes and update the date at the top of this page.
