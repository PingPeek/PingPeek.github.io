Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Core commitment: All network traffic data processed by FlowLens is handled locally on your device and is never uploaded to any external server.

1. Information We Collect

FlowLens does not collect any personally identifiable information. Data stored locally on your device includes:

  • Network traffic statistics (upload/download bytes, real-time speed)
  • Network simulation parameters configured by the user (bandwidth limit, latency, packet loss rate, etc.)
  • Traffic session records (stored in a local SQLite database on your device)

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • Contents or payloads of your network requests
  • URLs or domain names you visit
  • Account information, names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers
  • Device location information

3. VPN Permission

FlowLens uses the Android VPN Service API to create a local virtual network interface (tun interface) for intercepting, analyzing, and simulating network conditions entirely within your device.

This mechanism runs entirely locally and does not forward any traffic to external proxies or servers. The VPN permission is used solely to enable network simulation and traffic statistics features, and does not require root access.

4. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored in your device's private app directory (protected by the Android sandbox) and is inaccessible to other apps. Data never leaves your device through any channel.

Local data is retained permanently on your device until you clear the app data or uninstall the app.

5. Third-Party Services

FlowLens integrates Firebase Crashlytics and Firebase Performance Monitoring to help us improve app stability. These services may collect the following technical information:

  • Crash logs and stack traces
  • Device model, operating system version, and app version
  • Crashlytics installation UUID (a random identifier, not tied to your identity)
  • General app performance metrics (e.g., app startup time)

This data is sent to Google's servers and is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. No personally identifiable information (such as name, email, or location) is collected or transmitted through these services.

6. Data Deletion

All locally stored data can be deleted by clearing the app data in your device settings or by uninstalling FlowLens. No account or server-side data exists that requires a separate deletion request.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for general users and developers. It is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Policy Changes

If this policy undergoes significant changes, we will notify users through app update release notes and update the date at the top of this page.
